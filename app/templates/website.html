Portfolio
Aside from my GitHub, I have contributed to a few other projects. These are showcased here.
Shoot the Foe

Shoot the Foe was created at Valkyr Games in 2014 and 2015. It is a never-ending shoot-em-up where the player character's foes must be shot down. It launched on Google Play in October 2015. I programmed the entire game and designed a majority of it.

OxiGon

OxiGon was created at Valkyr Games in 2016. It is a simple game in which the player must defend their OxiGon against incoming attackers. It launched on Google Play in December 2016. I programmed the entire game and designed a majority of it.
